Bunnies!

Bunnies or better known as rabbits or hares

Learn More

Paws

Bunnies don't have very good pads on their feet making them sensitive to the surfaces they walk on

Diet

Rabbits should have a daily diet of mostly hay, a smaller amount of fresh vegetables, and a limited number of pellets.

Habitat

Wild rabbits can be found in woods, forests, meadows, grasslands, deserts, tundra and wetlands. Wild rabbits create their own homes by tunneling into the ground. These tunnel systems are called warrens and include rooms for nesting and sleeping.

Fun Fact

A baby rabbit is called a kit, a female is called a doe and a male is called a buck.


Media VS Fact

One of the world’s best-known rabbits is the Warner Bros cartoon character, Bugs Bunny — who’s often seen munching on a carrot! In fact, carrots aren’t a natural part of a rabbit’s diet and can give bunnies an upset stomach if they eat too many.
